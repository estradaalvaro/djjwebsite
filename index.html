Our Coaches

Alvi

Head Coach

Meet Coach Alvi, our Black Belt in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu with 11 years of training and 7 years of teaching experience. His journey spans Europe, Asia, South America, and Australia. Alvi combines martial skill with an intellectual approach, ensuring effective, enlightening training. He is a guest-coach at Freestyle MMA.

Miranda

Assistant Coach

Meet Assistant Coach Miranda, a blue belt with 3 years of training. She overcame challenges through Jiu Jitsu and is passionate about empowering women. Graduating from the University of Wollongong in Creative Arts, she is dedicated to fostering a supportive and inspiring community at Dapto Jiu Jitsu.

Aisha

Wrestling Coach

Meet Coach Aisha, our esteemed wrestling coach with over a decade of high-level experience in folkstyle, freestyle, and Greco-Roman wrestling. Trained under legendary Dan Gable, she brings unparalleled knowledge and dedication, showcasing the pinnacle of American wrestling prowess.
